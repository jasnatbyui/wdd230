ABOUT US: The Temple Inn & Suites is a hotel chain headquartered in Bethesda, MD that builds and manages specialized hotel accommodations near temple locations of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints around the world. We cater to temple workers, patrons, and their families by providing specialized services and accommodations that meet the needs of those patrons. The hotel accommodations and services are available to anyone willing to abide by the terms and policies. This hotel chain offers to meet the needs of temple patrons who come to serve in the temple or who participate in events such as sealings, weddings, receptions, and youth trips..
