We Love Timaru, and know that you will too. Below you will be inspired and attracted by the many awesome attractions and characteristics of Timaru, the Riviera of the South.

The main street of Timaru, has great shopping with a lot of heritage buildings in this older part of town.

The Timaru District Council building is a great example of heritage styled buildings. A great backdrop for a building.

The Port Loop is a spectacular looping road that allows vehicles to drive from the shopping centre down to the port area.

The Regent Theatre is the regions premier concert venue, you will love the decor and the venue is host to many performances.

This aerial view of Timaru shows how in the morning you can be surfing and in the afternoon you can go snow skiing at the mountains to the west. Surf and Ski in a day, yeah.

The Timaru District coastline is home to marine reserves, coastal walks and vantage points make this area a great place to spend a few hours, or take a swim or surf for more excitement.

Timaru has a great history, a lot of which is illustrated in it's older buildings. Timaru is a great place to live, the housing is affordable and of good quality.

Travelling to the west of Timaru the hills and mountains inland offer great views looking back to the city and coastline.
